Elewaiter

Product Innovation

Role: Creative group head

ELEWAIITER is a product which combines elevator and vending machine to solve the problem of getting something immediately without stepping out the stairs.


Insight:

My team thinks out it from experience: on a hot summer day, you want to buy a bottle of orange juice. Finding a convenience shop still needs more than 100 meters, which is too far; a vending machine is not easy to found, and ordering take-outs means more than half an hour waiting time.

Solution:

What if combining elevator, the vehicle delivering goods to every family, and vending machine which is the mini store providing varieties of goods.
Thus, we created "ELEWAIITER". Want to get something in a second? Just enter the "ELEWAIITER" and get it!

We call it Elewaiter, as the homophonic of "elevator". Just like your waiter, providing home delivery service.

In this way, the purchasing process will be faster and easier, meanwhile, obey the principle of least effort. We believe ELEWAIITER will be able to change people's lifestyle in the future.
