IOS GRAND

Product Innovation

Role:Ideation developer, video editor, copy writer.
Collaborator: Grace Ge

Insight:

Smart phones’ reaction are too fast and sensitive to keep up with for old people.And old people have hard time to start up using a smart phone.

Products: IOS Grand for Apple.Inc

IOS Grand can measure old people’s reaction speed and record their usage habits through artificial intelligence movement recognition and eye tracking.
Slow the interface jump to 1.4 seconds, understand the olds’ trembling fingers better, and offer patient guide to help them install new applications

Slogan:

IOS Grand for the old, making the smartphone to be smart friend.
