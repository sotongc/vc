A PENGUIN'S ADVENTURE

TVC

Role: Team leader, AfferEffect, animation editor.

Background:

QQ is the abbreviation of Tencent QQ. It is an Internet-based instant messaging (IM) software developed by Tencent.

Brief:

QQ makes connections more interesting.

Insight:

Through a story based on the QQ icon, a little penguin, this video shows how QQ connects people and changes the way people communicate.
