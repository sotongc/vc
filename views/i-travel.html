I-TRAVEL

Product Innovation

Role: Creative group head, video editor, illustrator.

Insight:

Many young people find the tour group boring and insipid, and the route of the Raiders is the same. They want to travel individually, and play in special local area.

Product:

I-travel App, a theme Travel Route Sharing Platform, encouraging people to share their travel routes by offering simple-used and pretty models. Without editing plenty materials, people can upload a special guide route based on the models.
In I-travel, routes publishers can be registered as tour guides by real name, and popular routes can also be developed by travel agencies based on the platform.
Whether the publisher can form a group, or a cooperative travel agency, carry out offline activities.
I-travel app as a platform, from which service fees will be drawn.

Demo:

The app will contain numerous travel routes with massive themes.
Such like, Movie theme tour. Follow Wang Jiawei’s films and wandering on the streets of Hong Kong.
Mahjong Teahouse Tour. Experience the authentic Sichuan Mahjong in the Chengdu Teahouse.
Local snack tour. Find the most rustic taste in the corner.
Silent journey, feeling the power of walking on the silent road.

Slogan:

I-travel. Love traveling. Don’t miss a good trip.
